Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

How do I book a trip?

Booking with TravelExplorer is easy! Simply browse our destinations, select your preferred package, click "Book Now", and fill out the booking form. You can also call us at +1-800-TRAVEL or email info@travelexplorer.com for personalized assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. We also offer easy EMI options for bookings above ₹50,000. All payments are 100% secure and encrypted.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before departure: Full refund minus ₹5,000 processing fee
15-29 days before: 50% refund
7-14 days before: 25% refund
Less than 7 days: No refund
We recommend purchasing travel insurance for maximum protection.

Do I need a visa for international destinations?

Visa requirements vary by destination and your nationality. Most destinations offer visa-on-arrival or e-visa for Indian passport holders. Our team will provide detailed visa information and assistance once you book your trip. We also offer visa processing services for an additional fee.

What's included in the package price?

Our packages typically include accommodation, daily breakfast, airport transfers, sightseeing tours, and local guide services. Specific inclusions vary by package - check each destination's "Package Includes" section for details. International flights, lunch/dinner (unless specified), and personal expenses are usually not included.

Can I customize my travel package?

Absolutely! We specialize in customized travel experiences. Contact our travel consultants to discuss your preferences, budget, and interests. We can adjust accommodation levels, add extra destinations, include special activities, or create a completely bespoke itinerary just for you.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer attractive discounts for group bookings. Groups of 6-10 people get 10% off, and groups of 11+ get 15% off. Perfect for family reunions, corporate trips, or traveling with friends. Contact us for a customized group quote.

Is travel insurance included?

Basic travel insurance is included in all our international packages covering medical emergencies up to ₹5,00,000. We also offer comprehensive insurance upgrades covering trip cancellations, lost baggage, flight delays, and adventure activities. We highly recommend upgrading for complete peace of mind.
